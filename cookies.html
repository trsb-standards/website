This website uses only strictly necessary cookies. We do not use analytics, advertising, or tracking cookies of any kind.

1. What are cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by a website. They are widely used to make websites function correctly and to provide information to site operators. Cookies set by the website operator are "first-party cookies"; cookies set by other parties are "third-party cookies".

2. Cookies we use

The TRSB website uses only the following cookies, all of which are strictly necessary:

NameTypeDurationPurpose
trsb_cookies_accepted First-party, localStorage Persistent Records your cookie consent preference so the banner does not reappear on every visit.
Session cookies (hosting provider) First-party, session Deleted when browser closes Standard session management required by the web server. Contains no personal information.

3. Strictly necessary cookies

Strictly necessary cookies are essential for the website to function and do not require your consent under applicable law. We describe all cookies we use, including strictly necessary ones, in this policy.

4. Cookies we do not use

The TRSB website does not use:

  • Analytics cookies — we do not use Google Analytics, Matomo, or any other analytics platform.
  • Advertising or targeting cookies — we do not display advertising and do not use any advertising technology.
  • Social media tracking cookies — we do not embed social media widgets that set third-party cookies.
  • Performance or preference cookies — beyond the consent preference described above.

5. Managing cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Instructions for common browsers:

  • Google Chrome
  • Mozilla Firefox
  • Apple Safari
  • Microsoft Edge

Deleting the trsb_cookies_accepted value from your browser's local storage will cause the cookie consent banner to reappear on your next visit.

6. Changes to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy if we change the cookies we use. The date at the top of this page indicates when it was last revised.
